 me

Hi! I'm Jorge and I was born in Dominican Republic a little bit more than 30 years ago.

When I finished high school, I had the chance to go to Germany as an exchange student. Coming from a developing country, Germany was a real shock. A burning desire for change got me back in DR, plunged headlong in Law School and Politics. For some reason, this is when I started building simple static websites. Corruption and disappointment made this passion for law slowly fade, giving way to my love for the web. I ended up being a Doctor in Law, MBA with a concentration in Marketing who proudly morphed into a Full Stack Web Developer...

I'm a MakerSquare Alumni (Cohort 3 - Austin, TX) and a lifelong avid learner. In addition to programming, I love reading and traveling and have had the opportunity to visit over 400 cities around the world, including 24 countries and 17 US states. I love animals as much as I love people and believe two things:

"It's better to be a motivated fool than a pathless genius."

"There's nothing more important than being happy and
nothing brings more happiness than chasing your dreams."
